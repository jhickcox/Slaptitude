About Slaptitude

Your attention span is directly related to your internal clock and its ability to know what a certain amount of time feels like. Through playful and fun reinforcement, Slaptitude encourages people to stretch their attention span, all while helping them focus in the here and now.

Challenge friends, challenge yourself, and have fun! Personalized feedback means people can discover what works for them and what doesn't, taking the guess-work out of productivity.

And remember,

focus...or else!

Medium Fi Prototype

This prototype is run using Marvel. Simply interact with it as if it were a mobile application. This prototype by no means represents the full functionality of our app. Due to the limitations of Marvel and the prototyping media, we could not simulate the timer functionality, so the app cannot actually track time and set off an alarm when the focus time is up. It also can't tell when a user switches out of the app (and therefore breaks their focus). The settings, progress, and leaderboard pages are all static and cannot update. On the user side, they cannot set their focus timer to a custom time, they cannot track their progress throughout the focus period, and they cannot specify their location. Lastly, the current design is in grayscale and hasn't had any color added yet, which means it's a bit boring. We're working on providing a fun color scheme to spruce it up!
